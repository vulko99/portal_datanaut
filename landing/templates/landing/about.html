{% trans "Why DataNaut exists" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Market data and technology subscriptions are one of the largest – and least transparent – cost lines in trading and financial firms. DataNaut was created to bring contract, usage and cost data into a single, trusted view. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Our goal is simple: cut waste without slowing down the desks, and give CFOs and investors a clean story on efficiency and control. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "The problem" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Trading and market data teams rely on dozens of vendors – Bloomberg, ICE, Refinitiv, EnAppSys and many more – each with their own contracts, entitlement files and invoices. Overlapping feeds, unused licences and renewals with little time to analyse options mean spend keeps rising while transparency lags behind. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Our approach" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} DataNaut is contract-first, not a data-reselling platform. We ingest invoices, contracts, entitlement files and usage data into a single schema, then normalise it into a single cost & usage repository. Dashboards and alerts sit on top, giving desks, CFOs and procurement a shared source of truth. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Why now" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Market data and technology spend keeps growing faster than revenues. At the same time, regulators and boards expect tighter governance and full auditability. With cloud adoption and stricter cost discipline, firms need a dedicated system of record for data contracts and entitlements – not just another Excel file. {% endblocktrans %}
